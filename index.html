How it work

Main steps for a good collaboration

  • meeting

    The process starts with getting to know my client and learning all-I-can about the project that I am going to work on. We will discuss strategies, target market, goals, and the anticipated size of the website. We will then provide you with an itemized quote.

  • research

    After gather enough information, I do a concept Web design layout and will adjust it until both sides, you and I will be happy that presents your business perfectly.

  • design

    And “old ways” desktop site is not good enough :). Today, a website need to work properly on any device, from desktop to smartphone.

  • development

    So, I got through the design stage and obtained the WOW-result:). Next, comes the part where I make things function. Coding and programming. I write clean, semantic, commented and W3C valid markup that allows others to easily understand and update content in the future.

  • release

    After thoroughly testing on a development server, we take it live! I will teach you how to use him and how you can measure the effectiveness of your site using web analytics software.
